# Instagram Carousel: SLMs for Southeast Asia
# 10 slides — hook → problem → solution → examples → CTA

## SLIDE 1 (HOOK)
Headline: "70 million businesses in Southeast Asia can't afford AI."
Subtext: Not "won't." Can't.
Visual: Bold white text on dark background. Maybe a map of SEA highlighted.

## SLIDE 2 (THE PROBLEM)
Headline: "The math doesn't work."
Body:
- GPT-4o: $2.50/$10 per 1M tokens
- Claude Sonnet: $3/$15 per 1M tokens
- A mid-size Jakarta company doing 10M tokens/month = $125-400/month
- That's someone's salary in most SEA SMEs.
Visual: Price comparison table or a simple cost breakdown graphic.

## SLIDE 3 (THE REAL COST)
Headline: "It's not just about money."
Body:
- When OpenAI goes down, your business stops.
- Customer data sent to servers in Virginia.
- Singapore PDPA. Indonesia PDP Law. Vietnam Cybersecurity Law.
- You can't control where your data goes.
Visual: Globe with data flowing from SEA to US, crossed out.

## SLIDE 4 (THE ALTERNATIVE)
Headline: "Small Language Models."
Body:
- 1-9 billion parameters (vs 175B+ for frontier)
- Run on a laptop. Or a phone. Or a $500 server.
- No API cost. No data leaving the building.
- Fine-tuned on YOUR data, for YOUR language.
Visual: Comparison — big cloud server vs small laptop. Clean contrast.

## SLIDE 5 (WHAT THEY CAN DO)
Headline: "They're not worse. They're specialized."
Body:
- Phi-3 Mini (3.8B) — runs on a phone
- Llama 3.2 (1B/3B) — open, free, downloadable
- Gemma 2 (2B/9B) — competitive with models 5x its size
- A 7B model fine-tuned on Indonesian customer support beats GPT-4o at that task.
Visual: Model logos with parameter counts.

## SLIDE 6 (SMEs)
Headline: "AI without the subscription."
Body:
- A warung in Yogyakarta: inventory + WhatsApp automation
- A garment factory in HCMC: quality control docs
- A logistics company in Manila: customs paperwork
- All running locally. All affordable.
Visual: Icons representing each business type.

## SLIDE 7 (EDUCATION)
Headline: "Tutors that don't spy on students."
Body:
- 50 million students in Indonesia alone
- Frontier API cost for one tutoring session: $125,000
- SLM on a school server: effectively free
- Student data never leaves the building
Visual: School building with a shield/lock icon. The $125K number is the shock factor.

## SLIDE 8 (THE BIGGER PICTURE)
Headline: "This is regenerative AI."
Body:
- Not just efficiency (fewer jobs)
- Ecosystem creation (more jobs)
- Indonesian devs fine-tune a model for legal docs → lawyers, notaries, legal tech use it → jobs created → value stays local
- That's what democratization actually means.
Visual: Circular ecosystem diagram — local devs → local models → local businesses → local jobs.

## SLIDE 9 (THE CHALLENGE)
Headline: "What needs to happen."
Body:
1. Language fine-tuning — Bahasa Indonesia, Vietnamese, Thai, Tagalog
2. In-region GPU infrastructure — shared clusters, government compute
3. Education — most leaders think AI = ChatGPT
Visual: Three icons representing each point.

## SLIDE 10 (CTA)
Headline: "The age of abundance might come someday."
Subtext: "In the meantime, let's build AI that works for the people who need it most."
CTA: Follow for more on regenerative AI in SEA. Read the full article → link in bio.
Visual: Clean, bold. Author name + putra.io.

- Colors: Dark bg (charcoal #18181B) + white text + accent purple (#C7B0FE) or teal (#0D9488)
- Style: Minimal, data-forward, no stock photos
- Each slide: max 40 words body text, big headline
- Consistent layout across all slides
- Slide 1 and 10 should be the strongest visually
