3. Architecture: The Antigravity Stack

Since we are using Antigravity, we will treat the platform as a system of autonomous agents rather than just static code.

A. The "Brain" (AI Integration)

Instructional Agents: Instead of static videos, we deploy "Socratic Bots" trained on course material. They don't give answers; they ask guiding questions.

Curriculum Agents: Automatically spider the web for the latest regulations (e.g., "Indonesia 2025 Carbon Tax Laws") to keep course content dynamic and up-to-date.

Grading Agents: Multimodal AI that can grade essays, code, and even video presentations (analyzing confidence and clarity).

B. The "Vault" (Blockchain Verification)

Standard: W3C Verifiable Credentials (VCs).

Implementation:

We will not just issue PDFs. We will issue Did (Decentralized Identifiers).

The "Green Check": A government official or HR manager can upload a TSEA-X cert file to a validator portal, which queries the blockchain (e.g., Polygon or a permissioned consortium chain like Hyperledger Indy) to confirm authenticity instantly.

Integration: A "Wallet" view in the user dashboard.
